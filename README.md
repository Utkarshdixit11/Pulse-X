
PulseX - Connected Healthcare Ecosystem

**PulseX** is a next-generation healthcare platform designed to bridge the gap between patients and doctors. It provides a seamless, unified experience where medical history is not just stored but visualized as a lifetime journey, powered by AI for predictive insights.

---

### Key Features

### 1. Dual Access Portal
A single, unified application serving two distinct user bases:
- **Patient Dashboard**: View personal health records, upcoming appointments, and AI insights.
- **Doctor Dashboard**: Manage patient records, quick-view summaries, and schedule management.
*Seamlessly switch roles for demonstration purposes.*

### 2. Lifetime Timeline
Gone are the days of scattered paper records. PulseX organizes medical history (e.g., "TB at 12 years", "Vaccination at 5 years") into an interactive, vertical timeline.
- **Visual History**: Scroll through a life's worth of health events.
- **Downloadable**: Export the complete autobiography of medical data instantly.

### 3. Gemini AI Integration
Leveraging Google's Gemini AI to transform raw data into actionable health intelligence.
- **Predictive Analysis**: Analyzes lifetime data to predict potential future health risks.
- **Routine Suggestions**: Generates personalized daily routines based on health history.

### 4. Interactive & Premium UI
Built with a focus on aesthetics and user experience.
- **Cinematic Experience**: Smooth transitions and animations powered by Framer Motion.
- **Modern Design**: Clean, minimal, and highly responsive interface using Tailwind CSS.
